Viventiva - Visualize Your Life in Weeks

See your entire life at a glance. Each square represents one week of your ~4,000 week journey. Track milestones, set meaningful goals, and live with intention.

Why Viventiva?

  • Life Visualization - See your entire life in a beautiful grid of weeks
  • Milestone Tracking - Record and celebrate meaningful moments
  • Goal Setting - Set intentions and track progress over time
  • Mood Journaling - Color your weeks with emotions and reflections
  • Privacy First - Your data stays yours, always

The Power of Perspective

The average human life spans about 4,000 weeks. Viventiva helps you visualize this finite time, inspiring you to make every week count. Based on the concept of "memento vivere" (remember to live), our tool transforms abstract time into a tangible, actionable format.
